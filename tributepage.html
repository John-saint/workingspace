GLENN FREDLY
Glenn Fredly went on air in one of the private television channel. One of the most simple yet beautiful among his pieces he sang was "My Everything".

Being a respected artist in Indonesia, Glenn Fredly is one of the most influential musicians, particularly within the spread of RnB genre. He started his career as a musician while he was young.

Glenn, as a musician, have done what he believes true within his heart by improving synergy within multi-cultural milieu around him. By being consistent and persistent, he became an inspiration forthe youngster on Malukuto create some musical art, and even be a musician.
Glenn had efforts when he tried to convince his hometown, Ambon, to become the world's music center. At October 31st 2019, he was working as a public figure striving his hometown to be designated for The Music Center of the World.
